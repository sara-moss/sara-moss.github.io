About Mrs. Moss

The Basics

I currently live in Carrboro, NC. In December, I started a new job as a virtual teacher to an awesome group of kindergartners and first graders at an online charter school in my home-state of Michigan called Uplift Michigan Online School. Although I enjoyed teaching in traditional brick-and-mortar schools for several years, I love teaching online.

Hobbies

My husband lovingly says I have a "hobby of making hobbies." This is definitely true in that I like to learn a lot of different things.

Here are my current favorite hobbies:

  • Sewing: After years of failing to figure out how to thread the bobbin on my sewing machine, I made it a goal during the pandemic to figure it out. I made a dress that was mostly wearable.
  • Furniture Restoration: I love finding worn out pieces of Furniture at the thrift store or next to dumpsters and giving them new life.
  • Chess: Like so many others during the pandemic, I watched The Queen's Gambit on Netflix an decided to learn to play chess.My husband had long been trying to teach me but I finally put in the effort and occassionally beat him!
