This Week in Social Studies

We will learn about our families!

Activities to learn more about our families

  • Make a family tree.
  • Interview a family member about their life. Ask them things like:
    • What did you play with when you were a kid?
    • What did you want to be when you grew up?
    • Has our family always lived here?
    • Do we have any family recipes?
